## 🚀 Do these now (highest impact, free)

### 1. Google Search Console — get indexed (15 min) ⭐ MOST IMPORTANT
Without this, Google may take weeks to find you. With it, days.

1. Go to **https://search.google.com/search-console**
2. Add property → **URL prefix** → `https://ecofiberbd.com`
3. Verify ownership. Easiest method for Cloudflare:
   - Choose **DNS record** verification → copy the TXT record →
   - In Cloudflare dashboard → your domain → **DNS** → add the TXT record → Verify.
4. After verified: **Sitemaps** (left menu) → enter `sitemap.xml` → Submit.
5. **URL Inspection** (top bar) → paste `https://ecofiberbd.com/` → **Request Indexing**. Repeat for `/products` and `/contact`.

### 2. Google Business Profile — win local + map results (20 min) ⭐
This is how you appear for "banana fiber supplier near me" / "banana fiber Bangladesh" in the map pack.

1. Go to **https://business.google.com**
2. Create profile: **EcoFiber BD**
3. Category: **"Fiber Supplier"** or **"Manufacturer"** / **"Wholesaler"**
4. Add: Dhaka address (or service-area if no storefront), phone `+8801672268121`, website, hours.
5. Upload your product photos (Grade A/B/C) + logo.
6. Verify (postcard, phone, or email — whichever Google offers).
7. Post your products and ask early customers for reviews — reviews strongly boost local ranking.

### 3. Bing Webmaster Tools (5 min)
- **https://www.bing.com/webmasters** → add site → import directly from Google Search Console → submit `sitemap.xml`. (Also feeds ChatGPT/Copilot search.)
